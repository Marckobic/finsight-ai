## LinkedIn Post (English)

---

I spent 3 months not knowing if I could pay my rent next quarter.

Not because I was broke — but because I had no clear picture of my runway.

I knew my bank balance. I didn't know how many months it actually bought me after taxes, expenses, and the unpredictable income of being self-employed.

So I built FinSight.ai.

It's a simple tool that gives founders and self-employed people one number that actually matters: **your personal runway** — how many months you can operate before you need to make a move.

Here's what it does:
→ Takes your gross annual salary + tax rate → calculates real after-tax monthly income
→ Factors in your expenses and savings balance
→ Shows your Personal Runway in months
→ Lets you run AI scenarios: "what if I cut $300/mo in subscriptions?" → shows exact months gained

It works in 2 minutes. No signup. No bank connections. Just honest numbers.

I'm looking for **10 founders or self-employed people** to try it and give me raw feedback.

What's working? What's confusing? What's missing?

👉 https://finsight-ai-tawny.vercel.app

Drop a comment or DM me if you try it — I read everything.

#buildinpublic #founders #startups #personafinance #runway

## Tips for Posting

**LinkedIn:**
- Post on Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10am or 12–2pm (higher engagement)
- Add a screenshot of the app as the first image (hero screen or baseline screen)
- Don't edit the post in the first 30 minutes (LinkedIn penalizes edits early on)
- Reply to every comment in the first hour

**Telegram:**
- Use the RU version for Russian-speaking startup chats (StopCorp, ProductSense, etc.)
- Use the EN version for international founder chats
- Post during business hours — don't post late evening
- Don't post the same message to 5+ chats in rapid succession (looks like spam)
- Best opening line: question or personal story, not "I built a thing"

**Chats to consider:**
- Founder/startup chats in your network
- ProductSense, Y Combinator Slack groups, Indie Hackers community
- Local entrepreneur Telegram channels
- Twitter/X thread version can be adapted from the LinkedIn post
